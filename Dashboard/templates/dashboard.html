About Us

Welcome to Internship Finder! This tool helps students and developers find the most relevant internships by scraping opportunities from trusted platforms like Internshala based on your preferred skills and criteria.

Developed using Python, Django, and modern scraping techniques, this project demonstrates the power of automation in career discovery. It was created as a learning project and continues to improve with new features!
